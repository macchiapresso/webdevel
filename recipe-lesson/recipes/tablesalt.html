Table Salt (NaCl)

(it is a recipe, after all)

Back to Start

This incredible spice will add the perfect saltiness to any dish in which it is used. The rich taste of Sodium, combined with the exquisite umami richness from the Chlorine, adds up to the perfect duo of the world of gastronomy and chemistry.

Ingredients

  • 1 mole ground Pure Sodium Cation (Na+)
  • 1 mole (22,4L NTP) Pure Chlorine Gas (Cl-)

Directions

  1. Add molten ground sodium and chlorine gas into a round bottom flask
  2. Beware of explosions
  3. Collect salt from the bottom of the flask
